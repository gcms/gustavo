Gustavo Cipriano Mota Sousa

Since September 2014, I am a PhD candidate at the University of Lille 1, working on the subject of self-managing cloud computing systems and software product lines under the supervision of Laurence Duchien and Walter Rudametkin.

I am part of the Spirals research group, a joint project-team between Inria Lille - Nord Europe and CRIStAL (UMR CNRS 9189) focused on self-adaptation for distributed and large software systems.

Before starting the PhD I worked for 8 year sas systems analyst and software developer in the public and private sectors in Brazil. From 2008 to 2010 I also taught courses on Programming and Computer Networking for students in Information Systems technical formation.
